Srishti Sethi SrishAkaTux

I’m a Learning Technology Designer/ Developer at the MIT Media Lab. At the Lab, I am part of the Learning Over Education Initiative, that seeks to develop new technologies for creative learning.

I have recently (June, 2015) graduated with a Masters degree in Media Arts and Sciences from the Lifelong Kindergarten group at the Media Lab.

It’s been so much fun treating Unhangout like a baby and seeing it growing over the past two years. The platform has over 12k users now, woot! I completed my Masters thesis around Unhangout, and now leading its design and development.

Before coming to the Media Lab, I was a Software Developer in India. While in undergrad, I was involved with an open source community, GNOME. As a Student Contractor at Google, I developed an engaging user-experience by extending GCompris to kids to help them explore a variety of activities theming braille.

When I'm not working, I'm caught napping under my work desk. To allure myself at times, I play ping-pong, do long bike trips, photography and make masala chai for friends.

It almost feels like home in Boston. Bikaner and Bangalore days are still being missed and cherished.
